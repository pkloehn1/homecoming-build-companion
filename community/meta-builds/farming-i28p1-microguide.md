## Summary

- Best smashing/lethal active farmer (Page 5+): Rad/MA Tank on map #54399 (10-min). Best AFK farmer: Elec/Rad Tank on maps #54401 (5-min) or #54400 (10-min). After Page 5 nerfs, S/L farming overtook fire farming for top performance, but the gap is small enough that switching isn't mandatory.
- Best fire farmer (still viable): Active = Rad/Fire Brute (map #41794, +4/8, AVs on); AFK = Rad/Fire Brute. AFK alt Stone/Rad Granite-less Tank is the best overall AFK fire farmer (no accolades or P2W needed) on the same fire maps #51289 (5-min) / #38973 (10-min).
- Earnings benchmarks (Page 5): ~3 AFK farmers ≈ 80M inf/hr; 1 active + 2 AFK ≈ 150M inf/hr. AFK fire took ~40% hit relative to Page 4; active S/L Rad/SS or Rad/MA tank stays close to pre-nerf throughput at ~3:00 clears.
- Build standards: All incarnates required, Alpha at least Tier 3 (level shift mitigates incoming KB). Bosses must be turned ON in difficulty settings or they spawn as LTs and inf drops to LT levels. Use the `FarmPro.mnu` popmenu bound to `e` to combine + eat reds for damage cap.
- Spines/Fire Brute is still viable (especially AFK); Quills/Spine Burst are 6-slotted with damage procs (procs out-perform traditional slotting here regardless of damage type). For long AFK runs (40+ min cave maps), swap one Spine Burst proc for Armageddon Dam/End +5.

Okay, so after a bunch of testing, the best active farmer is a Rad/MA Tank, and the best AFK farmer is an Elec/Rad Tank.

**_How To Fire Farm in Page 5_**

ACTIVE FARMING

Build: For Active Farming (i.e. you at your computer actively playing) use the attached build called "AA_ACTIVE_Page4". (All the incarnates in the build are required. Alpha slot being T3 most of all...as this will stop you getting knocked down.)

Map: #41794.

Settings: +4/8 Difficulty, AVs set to on. (This will keep the helper alive.)

How to Play: Empty slot in burn is for the perfect zinger proc. Just spam your AoEs and everything will die. There's a helper in the map who will summon phantom army's to help you bunch up the mobs. 1 x active farmer resetting every 3 mins + 2 x AFK farmers resetting every 5 mins = ~150 million inf per hour

MAKING YOUR OWN FARM

Not a fan of the meteor map? Pick whatever map you want and then give your mobs the following power selections: [screenshots provided for Minions, Lieutenants, Bosses (Active), Bosses (AFK)]

Then when you're in the farm, spam the "e" button to combine your inspirations into reds + eat them. (The "e" button will do both.)

Saw a bunch of posts in General Discussions saying farming is nerfed. So though I'd run the numbers to check.

Active 1 Account: Page 4 Rad/Fire Brute = 108mil p/h → Page 5 Rad/Fire Brute = 92.6mil p/h (~85% as effective). Solution: Switch to a Rad/SS tank and S/L farm. They can still clear meteor maps in 3:00.

AFK 1 Account: Page 4 Rad/Fire Brute = 52.8mil p/h → Page 5 Rad/Fire Brute = 31.8mil p/h (~60% as effective). Solution: Best I've been able to find is Elec/Rad tank. That pulls in ~33mil p/h (~63% of pre-nerf).

Triple-Box 3 Accounts: Page 4 = 1 Active Rad/Fire Brute + 2 AFK Rad/Fire Brutes = ~214mil p/h → Page 5 = 1 Active Rad/SS Tank + 2 AFK Elec/Rad Tanks = ~174mil p/h (~81%).

AFK 15 Accounts: Page 4 = 792mil p/h → Page 5 Elec/Rad Tanks = 495mil p/h (~63%).

So, pretty big drops across the board for fire farming. Single-account active s/l farming with a Rad/SS Tank is still viable. (But considering that Ground Zero having a target cap of 30 is a big reason for this, we're only one bug fix away from that going away.) The above numbers all rely on proc builds. So if procs get nerfed in a future update, farming will take another big hit.

New AFK Rad/Fire Brute build is attached. Elec/Rad AFK Tank is also attached. (With the tank, you don't need to toggle on Focused Accuracy.) The Active farming Rad/Fire in the OP is still good for Page 5. Haven't attached the Active Rad/SS tank because it's a friends build so need to ask permission first. Essentially just proc out everything including GroundZero/Radiation Therapy, and build for 130% base recharge. With hasten and FF active you're looking at 300% recharge.
