# Nic's Guide to IO's With IMPACT!

## Summary

- A handful of unique/global IOs (Panacea +HP/End, Miracle +Recovery, Performance Shifter +End, Numina's +Regen/+Recovery, Luck of the Gambler +Recharge) deliver outsized benefits when single-slotted into Health, Stamina, or any LotG-eligible defense power, with no need to chase set bonuses.
- Defense-stacking uniques Steadfast Protection: Resistance/+3% Defense and Gladiator's Armor: TP Protection/+3% Defense each grant a global +3% defense to all positions and types; combined in Tough they give +6% defense globally and remain active even with the toggle off.
- Knockback protection (-4 mag) is available cheaply via Blessing of the Zephyr, Steadfast Protection, and Karma KB IOs; one is enough for most PvE play, three stacked (-12) for those wanting heavier coverage, and -8 is a poor middle ground.
- Kismet: Accuracy +6% provides +6% to-hit (not accuracy) and is critical for snipers trying to reach the 22% to-hit threshold for instant snipes; its bonus only applies when the slotted power is active.
- Other strong picks include Shield Wall (+5% all resistance), Reactive Defenses (scaling resist +3% baseline up to ~10%), Unbreakable Guard (+7.5% Max HP, which also boosts heal magnitude), Impervious Skin (status resistance + non-stacking regen), Regenerative Tissue (+30% regen), and Preventive Medicine: Chance for +Absorb (scaling absorb shield as HP drops).

## Verbatim excerpt

Hi everyone! I know many of us love to delve into the intricacies of IO builds and try to eek out every iota of power we can. But there are also many who play that either have no desire to get involved with such things or feel overwhelmed by the possibilities and choices and so ignore this aspect of the game. This guide is more for the latter group of people but it never hurts to give it a once over even if you're an IO vet! This guide covers mostly unique, proc, or global benefiting type IO's. The main focus of this guide will be about which IO's can give you the most bang for your buck just by slotting them and without needing to worry about sets or synergy with other sets/powers.

All-Around-Good-For-Everyone-IO's:

Panacea: +Hit Points/Endurance — Unique piece of the Panacea PvP Healing set (10–50). Slot in Health at level 7 for a periodic tick of health and endurance for the rest of your days. Particularly nice for end recovery if you run toggle-heavy sets in the early game. Shines best in passives like Health or Fast Healing. Exception: when slotted into direct healing powers or Triage Beacon / Spirit Tree, this unique will proc on all allies in their area of effect.

Miracle: +Recovery — Unique piece of the Miracle Healing set (20–40). At level 17 slotting this in Health gives a welcome end recovery boost. When slotted into an active power (such as a direct healing power) this unique will NOT apply its benefit to the target but rather to the user of the power; effect lasts ~2 minutes and refreshes on activation.

Performance Shifter: Chance for +End — Proc piece of the Performance Shifter Endurance Modification set (21–50). Slot at 18 in Stamina; returns are about equivalent to having an End Mod SO in it. NOT unique — multiple +Recovery powers (Physical Perfection, Quick Recovery) can each take a copy. Endurance gained is based off 10% of total Endurance pool, so accolades that raise the cap (Atlas Medallion + Portal Jockey blue / Born in Battle + Marshal red, +5% each, capped at +10%) make the proc slightly stronger.

Numina's Convalescence: +Regeneration/+Recovery — Unique piece of the Numina's Convalescence healing set (30–50). Provides both regen and recovery. Useful for Blasters with sustain regen, Regeneration set users, Willpower, most Tankers, many Brutes. Slot in Health at level 27. Works best in passives; in active healing powers it applies to caster only, lasts ~2 minutes.

Luck of the Gambler: Defense/Global Recharge — Global piece of the LotG Defense set (25–50). At level 22 you can start slotting up to 5 of these into any power that accepts Defense Buff enhancements. Slotting one grants 7.5% global recharge — 5 slotted = 37.5% global recharge. Great to drop into the default slot of powers like Hover, Stealth, Combat Jumping, or Grant Invisibility. Globals are always active even if the slotted power is inactive.

Other Great IO's Whose Usefulness Varies:

Blessing of the Zephyr: Knockback Reduction — Global piece of the BotZ Universal Travel set (10–50), slotted into any universal-travel-accepting power as early as level 7. Provides −4 magnitude KB protection. For comparison, melee-set mez toggles typically have around −10. Recommendation: one −KB IO is enough for non-PvP; if stacking, jump straight to three (−12). −8 is not usually good because it doesn't cover much extra beyond −4. Always-active global.

Kismet: Accuracy +6% — Unique piece of the Kismet Defense set (10–30), slotted in any defense-buff-accepting power as early as level 7. Despite the name, it provides 6% +to-hit, not accuracy. Important for snipe powers — combined with Tactics or other to-hit buffs (Farsight, Targeting Drone, World of Pain) it can hit the 22% to-hit threshold needed for instant-fire snipes. Dominators have a harder time persistently hitting the number unless they take Link Minds from the Psi APP. Effect requires the slotted power to be active (passive in a passive, toggle-bound in a toggle).

Steadfast Protection: Resistance/+3% Defense — Unique piece of the Steadfast Protection Resistance set (10–30). Probably the biggest bang for your buck IO out there because it's uncommon-quality and provides 3% defense to all types and positions. Players whose primaries/secondaries don't include resistance powers often grab Tough from the Fighting pool to slot it. The +3% Defense is global and always active even if Tough is off; the resistance value benefits from being slotted in a toggle (which has a higher base than a passive).

Steadfast Protection: Knockback Protection — Global piece of the same set. Particularly valuable for Dark Armor and Fiery Aura users (no built-in KB protection) and Electric Armor (whose KB protection only works while grounded). Always-active global.

Karma: Knockback Protection — Global piece of the Karma Defense set (10–30). For non-melee characters looking to grab or stack −KB, drop in default slot of Hover or Combat Jumping. Always-active global.

Gladiator's Armor: TP Protection/+3% Defense — Unique piece of the Gladiator's Armor PvP Resistance set (10–50). Like the Steadfast Protection +3% Def IO, this is highly valued for the +3% defense to all types and positions. Stacking both into Tough provides a 6% global defense boost vs. all damage and positions. Even with SOs only, stacking these two can push some Super Reflexes Scrappers over the defense softcap with no other set bonuses. Always-active global.

Unbreakable Guard: +Max HP — Unique piece of the Unbreakable Guard Resistance set (20–50), slotted in any resistance-accepting power as early as level 17. Increases max HP by 7.5%. Because regeneration is tied to max HP, regen-based characters benefit doubly. HEALING values (yourself or others) are tied to YOUR max HP, so Empathy Defenders and similar healers also see increased heal magnitude. Always-active global.

Shield Wall: +Res (Teleportation)/+5% Resistance (All) — Unique piece of the Shield Wall PvP Resistance set (10–50), slotted in any defense-accepting power as early as level 7. Simple +5% global resistance to all damage. Always-active global.

Impervious Skin: Status Resistance — Global piece of the Impervious Skin Resistance set (10–30), slotted in any resistance-accepting power as early as level 7. Improves mez resistance (reduces duration; not protection) and also adds a regeneration bonus. Status resistance is a global and stacks with multiple instances slotted; the regen bonus is non-global, requires the toggle to be active, and only one instance applies regardless of how many are slotted.

Regenerative Tissue: +Regeneration — Unique piece of the Regenerative Tissue Healing set (10–30), slotted in any healing-accepting power as early as level 7. +30% regen buff while the slotted power is active (passive in a passive, toggle-bound in a toggle). In active heals, the buff applies to the caster only, ~2 minute duration, refreshable.

Reactive Defenses: Scaling Resist Damage — Unique piece of the Reactive Defenses Defense set (20–50), slotted in any defense-accepting power as early as level 17. Immediate +3% resistance to all damage; scales up as HP drops, peaking around 10%. Combined with the Shield Wall unique that's 8% baseline resistance to every damage type for two enhancements. Always-active global.

Preventive Medicine: Chance for +Absorb — Unique piece of the Preventive Medicine Healing set (20–50), slotted in any healing-accepting power as early as level 17. Periodically gives the user a decent absorb shield while injured; chance increases as HP decreases. Always-active global — slot location does not matter. Will nearly always proc the moment you res from defeat, providing a moment of protection on revive.
