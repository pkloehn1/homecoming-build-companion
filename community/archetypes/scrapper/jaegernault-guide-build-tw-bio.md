## Build philosophy (per the OP)

1. Take every Bio Armor power; treat the secondary as forced and focus enhancement choices on it.
2. On Titan Weapons, prioritise Crushing Blow, Follow Through, Rend Armour, Whirling Smash, Arc of Destruction, and Build Up. Defensive Sweep and Titan Sweep are levelling picks, skippable on the final build.
3. Soul Mastery is the chosen Patron because Shadow Meld is a no-crash 32% defence button, 15s up / 25s recharge, that lets the build sit at +4 with Incarnate-level enemies. Moonbeam is the slotted ranged option for runners and AV separation.
4. Slot for set bonuses to defence and recharge on the toggles; reserve purple/superior sets for the heavy hitters in the chain. Skip the pure-damage 6th piece of purple sets — diminishing returns make it wasted.
5. Genetic Contamination is enhanced for set bonuses and the knockdown proc, not damage. KD is "100% defence for 2 seconds."
6. Hasten is taken to keep the AoE chain and Build Up cycling; with set bonuses Build Up is up roughly every 15 seconds in combat.

## Per-power slotting (per Jaegernault's Post 1)

### Hardened Carapace (Bio T1, level 1)

- 4× Aegis (slotted for the smashing/lethal resistance bonus)
- Steadfast Protection — Resistance/+3% Defense (unique global)
- Gladiator's Armor — Teleport Protection/+3% Defense (unique global)

### Inexhaustible (Bio passive, level 2)

- Panacea — +Hit Points/Endurance proc (unique)
- Panacea — Heal IO (for +Max HP)
- Performance Shifter — Chance for +Endurance proc

### Environmental Modifications (Bio toggle, level 4)

- 4× Shield Wall (including Shield Wall — +Resist (All) unique, +5%)
- Luck of the Gambler — Defense/Increased Recharge Speed (+7.5% global)

### Evolving Armour (Bio toggle, mid-level)

- 2× Unbreakable Guard (incl. +7.5% Max HP unique)

### Ablative Carapace (Bio click absorb)

- Numina's Convalescence (slotted for the ranged-defence tail bonus); only recharge and heal matter inside the power, since absorb caps at base HP.

### Parasitic Aura (Bio T9 PBAoE heal/absorb)

- Recharge slotting only; heal slotting wasted because absorb caps at ~1300.

### Genetic Contamination (Bio damage aura)

- Slotted for set bonuses and the Avalanche knockdown proc, not damage.

### DNA Siphon (Bio click)

- 2× Recharge IOs as a panic button — set or proc-bombing not necessary by the OP's preference.

### Crushing Blow (TW T2)

- 6× Superior Scrapper's Strike (attuned), including the AT-set's crit proc.

### Follow Through (TW T5)

- 6× Superior Blistering Cold (attuned), for defence bonuses and the fast hold proc.

### Build Up (TW T4)

- 2× Recharge IOs (so it cycles ~20s with set bonuses).

### Rend Armour (TW T7)

- 5× Hecatomb (purple); 6th pure-damage piece omitted because of diminishing returns.

### Whirling Smash (TW T8 PBAoE)

- 6× Armageddon (the OP advised slotting the 6th despite Rule of 5 because the unique adds value).

### Arc of Destruction (TW T9 cone)

- AoE-set slotting (Obliteration / Avalanche frankenslot in later edits) and the Achilles' Heel/-Res style proc context. The OP notes AoD is the reliable host for the per-hit crit proc because the cone catches 5 targets.

### Defensive Sweep (TW T1)

- Levelling pick only; the OP drops it from the final build.

### Titan Sweep (TW T3)

- Levelling pick only; the OP drops it from the final build.

### Hasten (Speed)

- 2× Recharge IOs.

### Combat Jumping (Leaping)

- Luck of the Gambler — +7.5% global recharge unique.

### Maneuvers (Leadership)

- Luck of the Gambler — +7.5% global recharge unique.
- 5× Red Fortune for the defence tail (per the OP's general advice on Maneuvers).

### Tactics (Leadership)

### Tough (Fighting)

- Steadfast Protection +Def IO + Gladiator's Armor +Def IO (placed here per the OP's earlier note that the two +Def uniques live in Tough).

### Weave (Fighting)

- Luck of the Gambler — +7.5% recharge unique.
- 5× Red Fortune for ~7% defence + 12.5% Recharge (per the OP's pool overview).

### Boxing (Fighting)

- Empty / placeholder. Required to unlock Tough and Weave.

### Moonbeam (Soul Mastery)

- Slotted as a ranged attack / sniper utility for runners and AV separation. (Specific set unspecified by the OP.)

### Shadow Meld (Soul Mastery)

- 6-slot for max absorb [defence] and recharge; the OP explicitly calls this out as the "panic button" 6-slot. (Specific set unspecified.)

## Defence & survival posture (per the OP)

Idle baseline ~25.5% defence to all (Tough+Weave +13%, Maneuvers +5%, CJ +2.5%, P2W +5%); in combat the Bio toggles, Adaptation, set bonuses, and Shadow Meld push to ~70% to everything during a Shadow Meld window. Resistance leans on Tough + Hardened Carapace + Aegis bonuses + Shield Wall +5% unique; the Steadfast and Gladiator's Armor +Def uniques live in Tough. -Regen handled with Envenomed Daggers (300 stockpiled) and the Reactive Interface; -res handled with Achilles' Heel-style procs in chosen attacks.

## Incarnate plan (per the OP)

- Alpha: Cardiac (T3) early, then Musculature long-term.
- Judgement: Ion (the OP runs it as a "room cleaner" for minions over the aggro cap).
- Lore: Banished Pantheon T4 (immortal pet's PBAoE clears minions; the OP says T4 doubles DPS+ on big targets).
- Destiny: used heavily by the OP, specific slotting unspecified in prose.
- Hybrid: notes it boosts Judgement output.
- Interface: -HP debuff over Reactive (Reactive's 2.5% -res debuff is "naff" if you have alternatives); Reactive is acceptable if you accept the small per-hit -res adds up over the chain.
