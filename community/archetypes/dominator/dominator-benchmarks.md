## Summary

- The thread frames the question every endgame Dominator build needs to answer: what numerical benchmarks should I aim for once Incarnates are unlocked? OP self-resolves the perma-Dom math by pointing to MonteCarla's existing post and saves a movement-key bind that auto-queues both Hasten and Domination on directional input.
- Recharge benchmark: aim for **minimum 156% global recharge** for perma-Dom; add a smidge more if you cannot fit a Force Feedback +recharge proc anywhere in the build. Perma-Hasten is not a separate goal — it tends to fall out naturally once you hit the perma-Dom recharge target.
- Defense/resistance: **don't build for them** outside whatever armour happens to come with your chosen Epic Power Pool. Doms don't need defense, so the slot freedom should be redirected into **damage proc slotting** wherever the power combo allows.
- "Ranged Dom" is a trap — Black_Assassin notes that ignoring the much stronger DPA melee attacks costs significant damage. The recommended endgame Dom plays both ranges.
- Epic Power Pool tier list for Doms (per Black_Assassin): Fire (Fireball AoE + Melt Armor −res, takes Achilles' Heel), Ice (Sleet for high-uptime debuff + Ice Armor for resist/defense uniques + Hibernate for Preventative Medicine), Scorpion (Poisonous Ray for high-uptime single-target debuff, Achilles' Heel and multiple proc slots), and Energy/Psi Mastery for niche cases. Strongly recommended: the Teleport pool's **Fold Space** as a target-gathering tool for mez.

## Notable replies

- **@Black_Assassin (reply 3, 2026-04-11):** Materially answers the OP's four benchmark questions and adds three more (Epic pool selection, Leadership synergy, Teleport pool). This is the substantive guide content of the thread:
  1. **Recharge target:** "Aim for minimum 156% global recharge. Smidge more if you cannot fit a FFB: +rech anywhere."
  2. **Perma-Hasten:** "Not necessarily, but it might happen anyway." — i.e., it's an emergent property of hitting the perma-Dom recharge target, not a separate slotting goal.
  3. **Defense/Resistance for a 'ranged Dom':** "'Ranged dom' is going to be losing out if you're ignoring your much stronger DPA melee attacks… don't build for defense or resistance outside of whatever armour power happens to be in your epic power pool."
  4. **Slot freedom redirect:** "Doms don't need defense, so you should have significant room to boost your damage through damage proc slotting. YMMV depending on your particular power combo, but taking advantage of damage procs where possible is highly recommended."
  5. **Epic Power Pool guidance:**
     - **Fire** — great if you need a bit of AoE with Fireball and a strong AoE −res debuff from Melt Armor (also takes Achilles' Heel).
     - **Ice** — great for higher uptime debuff with Sleet; also get Ice Armor to cover all resistance and defense uniques, and Hibernate for Preventative Medicine.
     - **Scorpion** — great for high uptime single-target debuff with Poisonous Ray (Achilles' Heel and multiple other damage proc options).
     - **Energy & Psi Mastery** — also have their use cases.
  6. **Leadership synergy:** "If you have an assault with a snipe, Leadership works well since Tactics (and Kismet proc) will boost snipe damage. Also if your assault set has a build up with +special like Earth or Energy, then Vengeance can be lucrative."
  7. **Teleport pool:** "Strongly recommend teleport pool for Fold Space. Generally very useful tool, especially for gathering loose targets for mez."
