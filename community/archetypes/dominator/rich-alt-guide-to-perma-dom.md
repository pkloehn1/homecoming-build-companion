# The Rich Alt's Guide to Perma-Dom

## Summary

- Dominators have one of the largest gaps in the game between baseline and IO-kitted performance; with sufficient global recharge, Domination becomes permanent and the AT plays as a hybrid Controller/Scrapper/Blaster with mez protection.
- The build path slots attuned recharge sets early, layers in Hasten plus the P2W +15% recharge buff and the SG Base Empowerment +20% recharge buff, and reaches reliable chain-Domination by the early 30s and full perma-Dom (without manual Hasten) around 50.
- Recommended IO chase sets are Ascendancy of the Dominator, Basilisk's Gaze, Malaise's Illusions, Stupefy, Cloud Senses, Expedient Reinforcement, Crushing Impact, Obliteration, Decimation, and Sting of the Manticore — most chosen for +6.25%–+8.75% recharge bonuses plus Ranged Defence at the 6th slot.
- Defensive layering (Tough/Weave + Maneuvers + Combat Jumping/Hover + Link Minds, plus Steadfast and Gladiator's Armor uniques) gives ~25.5% baseline defence so a small/medium purple caps you, while Tough + Temp Invuln + set bonuses can push S/L resistance toward the 75% Dominator cap.
- Recommended Epic pools: Ice Mastery for near-perma Sleet, Psionic Mastery for Link Minds (+Defence and another LotG mule), or Primal Forces for Force Feedback +Recharge proc carriers and Temp Invulnerability toward S/L resist cap.
- Working backwards from the Rule of 5: 5×10% (purple sets) + 5×LotG +7.5% + 3×7.5% (Basilisk's Gaze ×2 + Manticore) = 110% global recharge, with 6.25%/5% sets layered on top to clear the perma-Dom threshold.

## Verbatim excerpt

### Post 1 — Section: Intro, Levelling Habits, Basic Setup, Power Pools, Epic Pools, IO Sets to Chase, Other Recharge Sources, Defence, Example Build (2023-12-22)

MonteCarla opens by framing Dominators as having one of the biggest gaps between baseline and IO-set performance. Base Dominators play like control-heavy, damage-light blasters with on-again/off-again Domination, but with set IOs Domination becomes permanent, providing mez protection, decent passive defences, and boss-level crowd control. The guide is pitched at players with "a couple of 100 million lying around" and aims to help them enjoy perma-Dom as early as possible.

Levelling habits: slot attuned enhancement sets as early as feasible with recharge as the exclusive priority and Defence secondary. Around level 30, chain-Domination starts to occur intermittently; by 40, perma-Dom is reliable if vigilant with Hasten; by 50, with a few purple sets swapped in, full perma-Dom that refreshes without manual Hasten clicks. Always take Hasten anyway for AoE controls and Build Up–style powers. Build for personal defence and aim to cap S/L resistance at 75%.

Basic setup: put Domination on auto, take Hasten early, and place both in a "utility tray" next to the main three trays. Useful binds:

    /bind q powexecname Domination
    /bind e powexecname Hasten

Auto powers don't always fire immediately and Domination can be lost from delays. Activate Hasten manually whenever it's up. Use the Powers→Combat Attributes panel to display Last Chance to Hit, Global Recharge, and Melee/Ranged/AoE Defence. Set Options→Windows→Powers Recharge Indicators to Bottom to see when Domination and Hasten are about to come back up.

Power pools:

- **Speed** — must have. Hasten is critical; even after perma-Dom, Hasten itself becomes permanent at the recharge levels involved, benefiting all long-recharge AoE controls and Build Up–style powers. Visual FX can be turned off at tailors.
- **Fighting** — Dominators get very good Resist/Def values from this pool, almost level with Tanks/Defenders. Kick is a write-off but Tough takes both +Def uniques (Steadfast and Gladiator's Armor) for ~6% straight away and helps reach 75% S/L resist with an Epic armor (60% slotted across both powers, 7.5% from P2W buffs, 7.5% from set bonuses). Weave adds 7% Defence and 12.5% Recharge with 1 LotG unique + 5 Red Fortunes.
- **Leadership** — almost always take it. Maneuvers ~5% defence slotted, takes a LotG and 5× Red Fortune. Tactics prevents blindness and helps attacks land. Vengeance opens up later as another LotG mule.
- **Concealment** — maybe. Grant Invisibility and Infiltration are LotG mules, especially if not going Psionic Mastery.
- **Flight/Leaping** — maybe. Hover/Fly or Combat Jumping for another LotG mule plus 2.5% defence.

Epic pools:

- **Ice Mastery** — Defender-level Sleet, around every 30 seconds (down from 120 base) given high recharge. Ice Armor caps S/L defence but loses the chance to cap S/L resistance.
- **Psionic Mastery** — Link Minds is another ~7% Defence, almost perma (can't be slotted for recharge, but is affected by global recharge); with a Power Boost–style power, becomes ~10%, plus a LotG mule slot. Indomitable Will mez protection is redundant under perma-Dom but capped Psi Defence + LotG slot are useful. Mind over Body is bypassed in favor of resistance armors.
- **Primal Forces Mastery** — Energy Torrent and Explosive Blast carry the Force Feedback +Recharge proc (35% and 18% trigger chance respectively) for +100% recharge for 5 seconds. Slot with FF proc + 5× Positron's, leaving out the Dam/Rech piece. Energy Torrent is Knockdown; Explosive Blast is Knockback (consider KB→KD). Temp Invulnerability gets close to the S/L resist cap with Tough.

IO sets to chase:

- **Ascendancy of the Dominator** — +8.75% Recharge and +3.125% Ranged Def at slots 5 and 6. Put in a Stun, Fear, or even an Immobilise.
- **Basilisk's Gaze** (Holds) — 4 slots give +7.5% recharge and 1.25% Ranged Def; good for both ST and AoE holds.
- **Malaise's Illusions** (Confuse) — +6.25% Recharge and +3.125% Ranged Def.
- **Stupefy** (Stuns) — +6.25% Rech at 5 slots, +3.125% Ranged Def at 6 (adds knockback).
- **Cloud Senses** (Spectral Terror) — +6.25% at 4 slots, Ranged Def at 6.
- **Expedient Reinforcement** (Pets) — +6.25% at 4 slots, Ranged Def at 6.
- **Crushing Impact** (Melee) — +5% at 5 slots.
- **Obliteration** (Melee AoE) — +5% at 5 slots, +3.125% Melee Def at 6.
- **Decimation** (Ranged) — +6.25% at 5 slots.
- **Sting of the Manticore** (Snipes) — +2.5% Ranged Def and +7.5% Recharge at 5 slots.
- **Dominating Grasp** has no recharge bonus — don't slot the set, but consider the unique Fiery Orb later.

Strategy: slot just enough pieces to claim the recharge bonus until the slot-rich late-30s/early-40s, then add the extra pieces for defence bonuses.

Other sources of +Recharge:

- **P2W buff** — +15% recharge, costs Level×Level×1000 per hour, up to 8 hours. The first batch (8,000 inf) lasts to ~level 20; the next (3.2 million at 20) lasts well into the 30s.
- **Base Empowerment** — +20% recharge for 90 minutes from a Base Empowerment Station. Bases TAXI-4000 or ZONE-8888 are recommended. Ingredients are any of: (Clockwork Winder, Boresight, Temporal Sands), (Alchemical Silver, Improvised Cybernetic, Symbol), or (Ruby, Kinetic Weapon, Destiny).
- **Force Feedback +Recharge proc** — +100% recharge for 5 seconds; unreliable but big, with high uptime when spamming AoE knockback (Primal Forces attacks, Psionic Tornado).

Defence baseline:

- P2W Buff +5%
- Fighting +13% (slotted Weave + Steadfast + Gladiator's Armor uniques in Tough)
- Maneuvers +5%
- Combat Jumping or Hover +2.5%
- **Total: 25.5%**, capped with a medium purple. Adding Link Minds caps with a small purple and probably caps Ranged at all times with the chase sets.

Example at level 27 (Pink Topaz, Earth/Energy/Primal Forces):

- Fossilize: Basilisk's Gaze ×4
- Salt Crystals: Sandman Heal IO
- Stalagmites: Ascendancy of the Dom ×6
- Earthquake: Rech IO
- Volcanic Gasses: Basilisk's Gaze ×4
- Bonesmasher: Crushing Impact ×5
- Whirling Hands: Obliteration ×5
- Power Up: Rech IO
- Total Focus: Crushing Impact ×5
- Fly: Fly SO
- Hover: LotG +Rech
- Kick: Range SO
- Tough: Steadfast & GA +Def IOs
- Hasten: 2× Rech IO
- **Defence: +13.5% (+19% Ranged); Recharge: +46.25% (+81.25% with P2W and base buffs)**

By level 35: 20%/26% defence, 68%/103% recharge — perma-Dom up if attentive to Hasten. By 50: +98%/133% recharge, with another ~10% from purples — perma-Dom without Hasten (or easing off P2W as it gets pricier).

Closing tip: the lifestyle is expensive. Acquire merits via Task Forces, Giant Monsters, story arcs; buy Enhancement Converters and list for 1 inf for ~1 million per 5 merits. Or use converters to upgrade common→rare crafted recipes for bigger profit.

### Post 5 — Section: Tank-style Variation Discussion (2024-03-29)

Responding to Starseed about defensive variation: MonteCarla varies between Dominators. Some are softcapped Defence with S/L resists, confidently tanking on Task Forces (e.g. Pink Topaz, the Green Lantern–like Earth/Energy). The current Arsenal/MA Dom doesn't take Fighting but still has some Leadership. ### Post 6 — Section: Perma-Dom Stress Test at 123.75% Recharge (2024-03-31)

Responding to Outrider's report of Domination dropping out: tested with Pink Topaz at 123.75% recharge buff (with Base empowerment). When Domination triggers, recharge is 1:29, and it can fire again without Hasten when idle as long as CoH has window focus — but Domination was lost while typing the reply. With Hasten up, it cycles fine. Conclusion: perma-Dom doesn't appear to be broken unless you're on a knife's edge with recharge AND you background the game.

### Post 8 — Section: How Domination Stacking Works (2024-04-01)

Domination can only be activated while the Domination bar is full, and the bar stays full while Domination is active. Therefore, if Domination recharges before it expires, it can be activated immediately because the bar is already full. You do have to build the bar first (about half a mission solo), but after that, with Dom on autofire and either pressing Hasten manually or running +123% recharge or more, Domination is never lost until logging off or dying. That's the beauty of perma-Dom — once recharge is high enough, no rebuild downtime.

### Post 9 — Section: Lag, Knife-Edge Recharge, Suggested 5% Buffer (2024-04-01)

The 123.75% knife-edge gives less than a second of reactivation window (recharge 89.3s vs 90s duration). This can fail due to lag: the server tells the client the power is ready, the client auto-presses, then a message returns to the server to activate. If either is too slow the server may get the message late. MonteCarla typically has Domination on auto-fire but also binds Q and E for Domination/Hasten because Domination has been seen un-greying without firing immediately. If perma-Dom is reliable with the SG base buff but failing without it, rebuilding for an extra 5% recharge is worth it: 87.4-second recharge with an extra Crushing Impact set gives 2.6 seconds of leeway instead of 0.7.

### Post 10 — Section: Rule-of-5 Working (2024-04-02)

Working backwards from the Rule of 5:

- 5 × 10% (Purple sets for Melee, Melee AoE, Ranged and Ranged AoE — or substitute Confuse for one of them — and Superior Ascendancy of the Dominator)
- 5 × LotG +7.5% (Maneuvers + Vengeance, Weave/Hover/CJ, and Stealth + Grant Invisibility or Indomitable Will + Link Minds)
- 3 × Other 7.5% (Basilisk's Gaze ×2 and Sting of the Manticore)

That's 110%. You can then add 3 × 6.25% (Stupefy, Glimpse of the Abyss, Positron's Blast, Decimation, Malaise) or 5% (Crushing Impact, Obliteration) to bring it to 125% or more. Not cheap, but doable. With the SG Base Empowerment buff, you can drop two purple sets.

### Post 11 — Section: Hasten Slotting Clarification (2024-04-23)

Responding to ItsDecoy's question about Hasten slotting: usually 2-slot Hasten, with 2 SOs while levelling and 2 Level 50+5 IOs once at 50. Perma-Dom is achievable without Hasten by stacking set bonuses, but it's tight and usually won't kick in until 50. Hasten gives breathing room to enjoy perma-Dom from somewhere in the 30s onwards.

## Notable replies

- **@Luminara (2024-04-02)** materially corrects the perma-Dom recharge math: Domination has a 1.32s animation, and powers don't begin recharging until the animation has elapsed. At 123.75% global +Recharge, Domination's recharge time is 89.39s, and 89.39 + 1.32 = 90.71s — slightly longer than its 90s duration. ~126.25% global recharge has been the minimum for true perma-Domination since Issue 5 (the theoretical minimum is 125.75% but no IO/buff combination produces exactly that). This refines MonteCarla's "knife-edge at 123.75%" framing — without external buffs (P2W, Empowerment Station, teammates), the true threshold is ~126.25%.
- **@Outrider_01 (2024-03-31)** raised the issue that some Dominators were losing the Domination bar between casts even at 123.75% recharge — this prompted MonteCarla's Posts 6, 8, and 9 above clarifying the failure mode is lag-related at the knife-edge threshold and recommending a ~5% recharge buffer.
