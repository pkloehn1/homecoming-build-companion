# For a new character leveling, how do you slot?

## Summary

- The OP (a new player on a SS/Invul Brute) reports a progression of SOs at 13/17 then crafted level-25 generic IOs at 23, and asks whether level-25 IOs can carry a character to 50.
- Sovera's recommendation: if budget is tight, run SOs until 22, then swap to generic IOs; by 22 most desirable IOs are available anyway.
- If money is no object, Sovera buys ~80% of the final build's set IOs and slots each as soon as the minimum level is reached, but personally avoids slotting most set IOs before 20–22 because the early-level versions are weak.
- Defense is the exception: Sovera prioritizes getting close to the softcap as early as possible, and will slam in ATOs at level 10 even before the general "wait until 22" rule.
- A level-7 IO gives ~10% enhancement where a level-10 SO gives ~33%, so SOs are usually stronger than low-level generic IOs in the early game.

## Verbatim excerpt

Original post (Its All In The Reflexes, 20 April 2026):

> Still very new, and I am curious how other people slot abilities when leveling a new character. Currently I am playing a SS/Invul Brute. For the most part, I ignored slotting in the early levels, and just slapped in whatever drops I got from beginning Atlas mission or DFB rewards. At lvl 13, I purchased appropriate lvl 15 SO's for all my important slots, and then upgraded them to lvl 20 SO's at lvl 17. Finally, at lvl 23, I crafted lvl 25 IO's for my important slots. I am currently lvl 29, and am told my lvl 25 IO's can last me all the way to lvl 50. Is this correct? Should I just slot lvl 25 IO's in everything, and forget about upgrades until lvl 50... or possibly lvl 47 if sets behave like basic upgrades? In your opinion, what is the best way to slot a character while leveling up?

Sovera's reply (21 April 2026):

If you have the money (which does not seem the case, so that's fair) then I'd grab 80% of the build's IOs and carry them with me and slot them as I reach the minimum level to do so. Until then I use SOs until 22.

In a min-max added wrinkle I'd not slot most IOs before 20–22 and would use IOs, but I don't care much (since it's just an hour and a half or so to get there) and other than defense (because I want to be as close to the softcap as possible) I start slamming in other stuff like ATOs at level 10. But a level 7 IO gives 10% where a level 10 SO gives 33% ish.

At 22 I'd swap to generic IOs, but to be honest by 22 most things I want to slot are available. I might not even slot anything if my late bloomer IOs are level 27 so I just wing those 5 interim levels with nothing in them.
