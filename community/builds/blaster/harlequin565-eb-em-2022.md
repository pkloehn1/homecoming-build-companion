## Context

Posted nearly three years after the 2019 Powerbolt build (see
[harlequin565-eb-em-2019.md](harlequin565-eb-em-2019.md)) — same character,
re-spec'd in Mids Reborn 3.1.2.5 after post-launch animation changes made Total
Focus a viable single-target finisher. Ranged-defense layer shifts to Artillery
in Energy Torrent; Tough is dropped to a 2-piece +Def-unique mule (toggle off);
Nova drops a slot to feed Total Focus; Sniper Blast and Energy Torrent each get
a Force Feedback +Recharge proc to chain into Hasten + Energize uptime. Final
defense numbers reported by author: 45% S/L, 43% Ranged.

## Notes from the author (if any)

Eight rationale changes the author called out versus the 2019 version:

1. **Tough toggle off.** Dropped to just the two +Def uniques (Gladiator's
   Armor, Steadfast Protection) and run with the toggle off. Whenever they were
   picking up enough aggro for Tough to matter, they were already in trouble
   that Tough wouldn't save them from.
2. **Total Focus picked up** as a swap-out for Energy Punch — the post-launch
   animation changes made it viable, and it hits very hard, serving as a boss
   killer or a "GTFO me now!" button.
3. **Energy Torrent runs Artillery** (a then-new set) for a bit more accuracy
   and ranged defense.
4. **Nova is sub-optimally slotted for damage**, but T4 Musculature pushes it
   back up.
5. **Hasten drops to two (50+5) slots.** Energize goes on auto because the
   author kept forgetting to click it on cooldown; Hasten gets clicked manually
   whenever it's up.
6. **Super Jump and Super Speed are each double-slotted with Blessing of the
   Zephyr** for ranged defense.
7. **Two Force Feedback +Recharge procs** go into Energy Torrent and Sniper
   Blast. KB is less of a problem in iTrials because almost everything is
   resistant to it.
8. **Slots pulled out of Stamina and Health** — they weren't needed. Health is
   "mostly fine, or at 0" and when in deep trouble, green inspirations are the
   lifesaver, not regen or Blaster resistance/regen.

Slottings have been shuffled to accommodate the changes; the author says it
plays "really smooth." Nova, Judgement, and Explosive Blast are all in the main
attack chain. Power Burst and Sniper Blast are the single-target rotation, but
with Incarnate powers flying around the author says getting into melee is
viable, so the two melee attacks get sprinkled in for fun. Final defense: 45%
S/L and 43% Ranged. Dropping the +Recharge IO in Torrent for a 6th Artillery
slot would soft-cap Ranged too, but the author says they don't really need it
given Incarnate uptime.
