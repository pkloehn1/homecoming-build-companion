## Context

Originally posted in 2019 as Powerbolt: Level 50 Magic Blaster — Energy Blast /
Energy Manipulation with Mace Mastery for Scorpion Shield. Standard P1-era
soft-cap-defense recipe: full Superior Defiant Barrage in Power Blast, Superior
Blaster's Wrath in Power Burst, Sting of the Manticore in Sniper Blast, Mako's
Bite layered into Energy Punch / Bone Smasher for melee-range S/L, and Luck of
the Gambler `+Def/+Rch` muled across Combat Jumping / Maneuvers / Weave / Scorpion
Shield. Tough runs with Gladiator's +3% Def and Steadfast Protection +3% Def
uniques plus a 4-piece Unbreakable Guard for resist + HP. Hasten is triple-slotted
with Recharge Reduction IOs, no Force Feedback procs yet.
