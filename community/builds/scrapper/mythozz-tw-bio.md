## Context

Captured as a standalone Mids' Hero Designer 2.22 forum-export block; no
accompanying parent guide was captured with it. This build skips the ancillary
pool entirely — instead spending late picks on a deep Leadership commitment
(Maneuvers, Assault, Vengeance) and a heavy LotG-mule layer (six pieces of LotG
in Maneuvers, six in Weave, plus Combat Jumping). The defensive layer is built
on Reactive Defenses 6-piece in Environmental Adaptation + Gladiator's Armor
6-piece in Hardened Carapace. Heavy hitters slotted procmonster: Hecatomb on
Rend Armor, Armageddon + FF +Recharge proc on Whirling Smash, Superior Critical
Strikes on Arc of Destruction, Superior Scrapper's Strike on DNA Siphon.
