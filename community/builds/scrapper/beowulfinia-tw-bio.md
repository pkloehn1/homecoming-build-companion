`HO:Centri` in Moonbeam, four uniques in Health (Panacea + Miracle + Regen
Tissue + an EndRdx-I in Sprint), Numina's Convalescence 5-piece in

proc in Follow Through, Armageddon 5-piece in Whirling Smash, Superior Avalanche
in Arc of Destruction, Obliteration 6-piece in DNA Siphon and Genetic
Contamination.
